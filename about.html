About Me

Welcome to my professional portfolio! I specialize in Geographic Information Systems (GIS), web mapping, and geospatial technologies. With a background in developing geospatial applications, I have worked on projects that span environmental, municipal, and global scales.

I focus on integrating GIS with cutting-edge technologies like agent-based modeling, hydrologic modeling, and artificial intelligence to solve real-world problems. Whether through creating web applications or building complex geodatabases, I strive to bring spatial intelligence into decision-making processes.
